How we calculate
1. Nisab = Silver price per Tola ÷ 11.66g × 612.36g
2. Zakatable Wealth = Total Savings + Money Lent − Money Borrowed
3. Hawl = Wealth must remain above Nisab for 1 lunar year (354 days)
4. If Wealth ≥ Nisab & Hawl complete → Zakat = Wealth × 2.5%
